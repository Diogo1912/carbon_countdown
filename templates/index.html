Welcome to Carbon Countdown

What you see up here is the remaining time we have to cut our carbon emissions by half if we want to revert global warming.
Sure this looks morbid and scary, but that's the point. The time we have left is getting smaller and smaller.
The UN has released a statement that we have until 2030, it is on that statement that our countdown is based on.
It is not meant to be taken literally, but instead as a symbol and reminder of the crisis we are facing and how it is urgent to act quickly and effectively.
Explore this website to learn more about the crisis we are facing as well what you can do as an individual to fight it.
Thank you for reaching the first step of your journey by informing yourself!

About Us

Carbon Countdown is dedicated to raising awareness about climate change and carbon emissions. We provide data and resources to help you inform yourself about the crisis and learn how to help fix it.
